### **1. OLTP systems are primarily designed for:**

A. Historical trend analysis
B. Real-time transaction processing
C. Data mining
D. Multi-dimensional analysis

**Answer: B. Real-time transaction processing**

---

### **2. OLAP systems are mainly:**

A. Application-oriented
B. Subject-oriented
C. Transaction-oriented
D. User-login oriented

**Answer: B. Subject-oriented**

---

### **3. Which of the following is NOT a characteristic of OLTP?**

A. Fast inserts and updates
B. High data cleaning requirements
C. Multi-user support
D. Real-time operations

**Answer: B. High data cleaning requirements**

---

### **4. OLAP typically pulls its data from:**

A. ATM machines
B. Operational databases
C. Data warehouses or data marts
D. User interfaces

**Answer: C. Data warehouses or data marts**

---

### **5. In OLTP, expensive queries should be avoided because:**

A. They reduce security

**Answer: B. They slow down transactions**
